# Background Music for Video Reels

Place 4 royalty-free MP3 tracks here. They are mixed into every NanaBanana Reel
at 40% volume with a 1-second fade-out. If a file is missing, the video is silent.

## Required files

| File | Mood | Used for |
|---|---|---|
| `upbeat.mp3` | Bright, fast-paced | Job finished, team spotlight |
| `warm.mp3` | Positive, gentle | Got a review, community |
| `friendly.mp3` | Light, informative | Share a tip, FAQ |
| `energetic.mp3` | Urgent, driving | Seasonal, promotion |

## Where to get them (free for commercial use)

**Pixabay Music** — https://pixabay.com/music/
Search for: upbeat corporate, warm inspiring, friendly background, energetic driving
Download as MP3. No attribution required.

**YouTube Audio Library** — studio.youtube.com → Audio Library
Filter by: No attribution required. Pick 30-60 second instrumentals.

## Requirements
- Format: MP3
- Duration: 30 seconds minimum (FFmpeg trims to 7s automatically)
- Type: Instrumental only (no vocals — competes with the text bars)
- BPM: 100-130 for upbeat/energetic, 70-90 for warm/friendly
